# DISPLACE 2023 Challenge: Tackling Multilingual and Multi-Speaker Diarization in Code-Mixed Speech

The DISPLACE 2023 Challenge focuses on advancing speech technology to handle multilingual, multi-speaker conversations, particularly in code-mixed speech environments. This first-of-its-kind task benchmarks both speaker and language diarization on the same dataset, highlighting challenges unique to multilingual, code-mixed conversations. Participants work on improving speaker diarization (SD) and language diarization (LD), where systems must navigate speaker switches alongside language code-switching. The challenge dataset consists of natural multilingual, multi-speaker conversations evaluated on single-channel far-field recordings, with a baseline system provided for benchmarking.
